Our Story

BUILT IN
THE HALLS

Voltage started in 2026 when a group of high school sophmores got tired of wearing the same cookie-cutter fits as everyone else. We wanted gear that actually hit — clothes that looked good in the hallways, at the game, and everywhere in between.

Every piece we drop is produced by people who actually go to school. We test everything ourselves. If it doesn't pass the hallway test, it doesn't make the cut.
